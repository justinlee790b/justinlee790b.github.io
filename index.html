About Me

Heya! My name is Justin Lee, a Korean American QA Analyst based in Metairie, Louisiana. I currently work at Testronic Laboratories, where I own QA coverage to ensure a smooth and balanced gameplay experience.

I bring experience across multiple QA roles — from managing test cases and databases to leading comprehensive test passes for unreleased Unreal Engine projects. My work blends technical precision with a deep understanding of player expectations, shaped by years of competing at a high level in Valorant and fighting games like Skullgirls.

I’m passionate about quality, competitive integrity, and creating polished, player-first experiences. With a B.S. in Computer Science from California State University, Northridge, and an ISTQB Foundation certification, I combine structured testing methodology with an authentic love for gaming and improvement through iteration.

When I’m not working, you can usually find me traveling across the country to compete at major fighting game tournaments!
